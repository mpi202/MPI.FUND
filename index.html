About

Marketprog Asset Management Zrt.

MARKETPROG Asset Management Zrt. is a Hungary-based, MNB-supervised asset manager (AIFMD). Established in 2014, part of the Primus Wealth group.

Who We Are

Marketprog Asset Management is a Hungary-based and regulated asset manager according to AIFMD, offering fund and portfolio management, fund set-up, fund administration, custody, capital introduction, and investment advisory. We manage 32 investment funds in 17 families across HUF, EUR, and USD share classes.

Our Edge

Proprietary analytical tools developed in-house, combined with deep experience in Central European fixed income and derivative markets. Our team brings institutional-grade processes to every portfolio.
